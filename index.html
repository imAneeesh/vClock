How to use the online alarm clock
Set the hour and minute for the online alarm clock. The alarm message will appear once you press "+" button, and the preselected sound will be played at the set time. You can also set repeated alarm on desired days of week. The online alarm clock will not work if you close your browser or shut down your computer, but it can work without an internet connection.
